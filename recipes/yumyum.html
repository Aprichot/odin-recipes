YumYum Deviled Eggs

While scavenging in a delapitated building infested with radroaches, you come across a broken refrigerator and uncover a box of YumYum Deviled Eggs inside! Don't worry about that pesky ticking coming from your geiger counter, the radiation adds flavor!

Ingredients
  • 6 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp miso paste
  • 1/4 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/4 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp ground mustard
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 tsp ground fennel
  • salt and pepper to season
  • fresh chives

Instructions

  1. Place the eggs in a pot and fill with enough water to completely cover all the eggs. Cover with a lid and turn heat to over medium-high heat. Bring the water to a boil and start a timer for 8 minutes.
  2. Once the timer for the eggs has gone off, immediately take the pot off the stove and place it under cold running water. Move the contents to a bowl with ice cubes and water. Carefully remove the shell from the eggs.
  3. Cut the eggs in half. Place the egg yolks in a bowl with the mayonnaise and miso. Combine the ingredients until smooth. Add the coriander, cumin, turmeric, mustard, cayenne pepper, and fennel. Flavor with salt and pepper to your liking.
  4. Spoon the egg yolk mixture into each of the egg whites. Garnish with fresh chives.
